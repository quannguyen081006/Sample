Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:

Cook spaghetti. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy. Beat eggs and mix with cheese. Combine hot pasta, pancetta, and egg mixture. Mix well and serve with black pepper.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

Cream butter and sugars. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix in dry ingredients, then fold in chocolate chips. Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10-12 minutes.

Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions:

Slice mozzarella and tomatoes. Layer with basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
